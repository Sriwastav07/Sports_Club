About Sports Club

At Sports Club, an effort has been made to provide world-class facilities and infrastructure for promotion of sports and health. The club has a swimming pool of international standards a fully equipped health club, six acres of open ground for promoting outdoor sports like cricket, football, volleyball, basketball, lawn tennis etc. and has facilities for indoor games like badminton, squash, billiards, snooker, table tennis, chess, carroms, skating, pistol shooting, gymnastics, etc.
